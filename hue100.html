FM 100 HUE TEST

The Farnsworth-Munsell 100 hue test is one of the most famous color vision tests available. It belongs to the group of hue discrimination, also called arrangement tests.

The overall error score relates more or less to the severity of your color vision deficiency. It could be shown that highly motivated people can improve their result up to 30% and if you get to know the test, your result will also improve to a certain degree.

How the test works:
